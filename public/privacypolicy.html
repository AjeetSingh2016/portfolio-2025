Privacy Policy

Thank you for choosing Taskify, a task management application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with applicable privacy laws, including the GDPR, CCPA, CPRA, and CalOPPA. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we do not collect and how we protect your privacy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Taskify does not collect any personal information or sensitive data from its users. We do not gather, store, or share any personal data, such as your name, email address, or any other identifiable information. No personally identifiable information is collected for any purpose.

2. Use of Personal Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use or process any personal data. Taskify is a simple task management application that does not require or involve the processing of user data.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your data to third parties. As we do not collect personal data, there is no data to be shared or sold.

4. Third-Party Services

Taskify does not use any third-party services for analytics, advertising, or data processing. The app is fully self-contained, and no third-party tools are integrated into the app.

5. Data Security

Since Taskify does not collect or store any personal information, there are no security concerns related to user data. However, we recommend keeping your device secure by using proper security measures like passwords and biometric authentication.

6. Users' Rights Under GDPR

Although Taskify does not collect personal data, if you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

  • Right of Access: The right to request copies of your personal data (Note: Taskify does not collect personal data).
  • Right to Rectification: The right to request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information (Note: No personal data is collected).
  • Right to Erasure: The right to request that we delete your personal data (Note: No personal data is collected).
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: The right to request the restriction of processing of your personal data (Note: No personal data is processed).
  • Right to Data Portability: The right to request that we transfer the personal data you have provided to us to another organization (Note: No personal data is collected).
  • Right to Object: The right to object to the processing of your personal data (Note: No personal data is processed).

7. California Privacy Rights (CCPA, CPRA, CalOPPA)

If you are a resident of California, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), and California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA):

  • Right to Know: You can request to know what personal information we have collected about you (Note: Taskify does not collect personal information).
  • Right to Delete: You can request that we delete the personal information we have collected about you (Note: Taskify does not collect personal information).
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sales: Taskify does not sell personal information, so there is no need to opt-out of sales.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: You have the right not to be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights.
